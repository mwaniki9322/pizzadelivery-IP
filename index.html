ABOUT US

Pizza Hub is a family business located in Nairobi, Kenya. We offer traditional Neapolitan style pizza made from nothing but fresh and local ingredients! Our recipes have been tried and tested for three generations. All our pizzas are cooked in our custom made wood fired oven which was designed and manufactured in Italy.
Feel free to visit us at Junction Mall, Along Ngong Road, Nairobi to have a taste of our pizzas! Can't make it? Feel free to place an order online.

Menu

MUSHROOM AND ONIONS

These two savory toppings create a delicious, aromatic and robustly-exquisite pizza that might send the more risk-averse pizza lovers running but rewards the gourmet types among us.

DOUBLE CHEESE

This is one of the most popular pizza flavors all across the globe, preferred particularly by vegetarians that comes with a double thick layer of cheese. It is even preferred by cheese lovers. Extra layers of cheese Yumm!!

PERI-PERI CHICKEN

Peri Peri Chicken is a flavorful meal of any cut of chicken that is marinated in and also served with a homemade peri peri sauce, a hot pepper sauce from south Africa.Topped with a swirl of our addictive peri peri sauce.

HAWAIIAN/PINEAPPLE

Pineapple pizza is, like BBQ chicken, one of the more controversial choices in the pizza world.Come enjoy this delicious pizza at Pizza Hub

BBQ CHICKEN

First invented at California Pizza Kitchen, this variety of pizza sees Hot BBQ Chicken sliced up and spread on the pizza, paired with drizzles of barbecue sauce

PEPPORONI

A classic. Just throw a few (or a ton of) slices of pepperoni on top of the cheese, and you’ll soon have a greasy, slightly spicy and delicious pizza that you simply can’t go wrong with.
